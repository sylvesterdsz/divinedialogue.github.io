Divine Dialogue

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2025

Privacy First: Divine Dialogue is designed with your privacy as our top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your conversations, prayers, and interactions remain completely private on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Divine Dialogue is built with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect any personal information from users, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Voice recordings or transcripts of your prayers
  • Conversation history or chat logs
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Account information (no accounts are created)

2. How Your Data Stays Private

Local Storage Only

All conversations, prayers, and interactions with Divine Dialogue are stored exclusively on your iOS device. No data is ever uploaded to our servers or transmitted to any external services.

Offline Functionality

Divine Dialogue operates entirely offline after the initial AI model download. Your prayers and conversations never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and confidentiality.

AI Model Download

When you first use the app, it downloads the Llama-3.2-3B-Instruct-4bit AI model from Hugging Face (https://huggingface.co/mlx-community/Llama-3.2-3B-Instruct-4bit). This is a one-time download that enables the app to function offline. We alert users about this download, especially when using mobile data, and recommend using Wi-Fi.

3. Third-Party Services

Divine Dialogue does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or social media networks. The app operates independently without external dependencies after the initial model download.

4. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • iOS encryption and security protocols
  • Your device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Apple's App Store security standards

5. Children's Privacy

Divine Dialogue does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, this policy applies to users of all ages.

6. Data Retention

All conversations and data are stored locally on your device for as long as you choose to keep the app installed. You can delete all data by uninstalling the app from your device. We do not retain any user data on our servers.

7. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data since it never leaves your device:

  • Access: All your data is accessible to you through the app interface
  • Correction: You can edit or delete conversations within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Your data remains on your device and is not transferred elsewhere

8. International Data Transfers

Since Divine Dialogue does not collect or transmit any personal information, there are no international data transfers to disclose.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date and notify users through the app or App Store update notes. Continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: sylvester.d@gmail.com

We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly and transparently.

11. Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to the Australian Privacy Principles and international privacy standards. Our privacy-first approach ensures we exceed most privacy requirements by not collecting personal information at all.

Summary: Divine Dialogue respects your privacy by design. We don't collect your personal information, we don't track your usage, and we don't share your data with anyone. Your spiritual journey remains completely private between you and your device.
